The 1993 Chrysler New Yorker Fifth Avenue was the last year for the model to use the iconic New Yorker style. In 94' Chrysler opted to chage to the less popular more aerodynamic style that ultimatley resulted in the death of the New Yorker line. In my opinion the 93' model was the best of all the designs over the course of its 56 year long life. The 1993 Chrysler New Yorker fifth avenue is my favorite car.
